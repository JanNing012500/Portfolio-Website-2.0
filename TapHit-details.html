TapHit

Overview

  • Engine: UE5
  • Team Size: 3
  • My Roles: Game Designer, UI/UX Designer, Programmer
  • Project Date: Dec 2022 - Jan 2023
    (Paused Development)

    • Thrilling arcade adventure with musical puzzles, power-ups, and endless challenges.

    Inspired by an old mobile game: Smash Hit


Summary

Tap Hit is an arcade-style puzzle game that challenges players to destroy barrels and solve challenges in real time. Featuring power-ups like Multi-Ball, Size Up, and Teleport.
Players must stragetically navigate through various levels and activate buttons to advance while also keeping track of their shots, as the game ends once they run out.

WIP Gameplay Demo



Role Breakdown

  • Collaborated with a colleague to develop game mechanics, power-ups, and progression systems for Tap Hit
  • Designed and implemented an interactive main menu that used game mechanics as a tutorial
  • Created a player progression system using blueprints and C++ to enable players to unlock perks and progress further in the game
  • Developed a Figma greybox prototype of the potential shop & advancement perks
  • Experimented with and developed two different types of input: aim-to-shoot versus tap-to-shoot and conducted playtests to determine which type of gameplay mechanic players preferred
