    • WanQuest is a turn-based RPG featuring three player characters who fight together against a golem enemy.


Summary

Created in Unity, the game includes classic RPG mechanics such as a set of moves and individual inventories with items that can be used in combat. Players select skills or items to use during their turn, and the golem selects a skill to use at random. The game will then calculate the order in which the skills will be executed based on their speed value, rather than immediately carrying out the chosen skills. The battle ends when one side's health pool is reduced to zero. This speed mechanic in WanQuest adds an element of risk to combat, as the player must balance the potential consequences of slower, higher damage moves versus quicker, lower damage moves. When a player chooses to use abilities that have a faster speed, they may not be nearly as effective, but on the other hand, players choosing slower moves carries the risk of taking more damage or even dying before their selected ability is even executed. This speed element forces the player to carefully consider the risks of each of their choices depending on the situation.
