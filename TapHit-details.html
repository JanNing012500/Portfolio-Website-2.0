Summary

Tap Hit is an arcade puzzle-action game that combines fast-paced gameplay with precision. Players earn points by destroying barrels and solving challenges in real time across various levels. Each level features its own set of challenges which the player must navigate through. The game features a range of power-ups, including the Multi-Ball, which shoots out three balls in a volley, and the PowerShot, which significantly increases the power and speed of your shots.

Players can also use the Teleport power-up to avoid obstacles or transport to a different area of the level if they get stuck on a tricky puzzle. To advance to the next area of the level, players must activate two buttons at each gate by shooting at them. However, they must be mindful of how many shots they have left as the game will end once you run out of shots. Utilize the power-ups wisely and aim for victory in Tap Hit!
