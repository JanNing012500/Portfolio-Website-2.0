E-commerce site - CardsCoinsCollectables



Introduction

After much trial-and-error with several e-commerce sites, the limitations forced upon sellers in terms of customizability of their shops frustrated me as I wanted to provide the best shopping experience for my customers. This project demonstrates my UI/UX design skills and ability to create visually appealing, user-friendly websites that drive sales. This site provides a seamless shopping experience on both desktop and mobile devices, allowing customers to easily browse products and make purchases.


Goals and Objectives

As with all online shopping sites, the ultimate goal is to increase traffic and sales which I strived to do through improving my website’s UX.
By providing customers with a website that’s not only easy to navigate, but also visually appealing, they’re much more likely to complete their purchase and revisit if they enjoyed their shopping experience.


Problem and Solution



  • Decreased the number of abandoned checkouts through the use of the 60-30-10 rule, making it easy to guide the customer to the checkout screen after adding their products to their carts.
  • With the inclusion of our Live Chat which featured instant Frequently Asked Questions page, customers gain instant answers to most of their questions. The Live Chat also made it more convenient for customers to ask questions and for me to answer them; the whole process included fewer steps for both parties compared to email.
  • Since almost half of all online sales are made on a mobile device, making the site mobile-friendly helped boost sales for those who prefer to shop on their phones.

Design Direction



I decided on the third low-fidelity home screen option as it features a large banner showcasing the main product with a clear call-to-action button in the center. The large banner immediately draws the attention of visitors, while the prominent placement of the buy now button encourages them to make a purchase rather than abandoning their cart.
The visual hierarchy of the products provides visitors with a glimpse of other items of interest, making it more likely for them to continue browsing and add additional items to their cart. Overall, this layout is an effective way to optimize the user experience and increase conversion rates.


Conclusion

Seeing the changes and design choices I made and implemented have a positive impact on my site was a very rewarding process. I was not only able to create a custom site where I had control of every element, but the final result lead to significantly lower abandoned checkout, increased mobile & desktop sessions, higher customer retention rate, and positive feedback from customers regarding their shopping experience.
